Black Rhino

The black rhinoceros or hook-lipped rhinoceros is a species of rhinoceros, native to eastern and southern Africa.

  • Scientific Name: Diceros bicornis
  • Average Length: 3 to 3.75 m
  • Average Lifespan: 35 to 50 years
  • Habitat: Thick scrub and bushland

Although the rhinoceros is referred to as black, its colors vary from brown to grey.
